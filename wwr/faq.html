Q: How much does it cost?

A. We charge $20/ person youth under 13 are $10.

Q. What should I bring?

A. You should bring a change of clothes, towel, water shoes or sandals with water holes.

Q. Do I need experience?

A. No experience is necessary, our guides will teach you everything you need to know.

Q. What is the age requirement?

A. The minimum age is 8 years old. We also do not reccomend going if pregnant.

Q. How long does a trip last?

A. Most trips last between 2-3 hours depending on the river conditions.
